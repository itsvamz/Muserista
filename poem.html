She Breathes
Running as fast as a tiger;
Burning in huge flames of her own fire.
Chasing her dreams like a cat after a mouse;
Surpassing every hurdle with a lion’s pounce.


A crown always being adorned on her head;
Her smile stays even after her failures append.
Never noticed a single tear sliding down her eyes;
The depth in those orbs have multiple stories that shine.


Taking a hault from her sprint;
She breathes, breathes and breathes.
Reflecting on her journey so far;
A contented sigh is all she releases.


Remembering about her long way ahead;
Bringing a lovely smile back on her face.
Coming back to the position of a head-set;
She again gets on the race.

The great things to be achieved
Are bound to take miles.


Taking notes from behind

In order to proceed the show.


Advancing towards the next part of the book
With an owe to never let the smile go;
Leaving out all the tears off the hook

Individuality being the first trait
Starting the journey with no hate;

Solo Explorer
Sailing in a boat in an empty sea
She rows the water with such glee;
Looking at the sun in an empty sky
She assures herself of the upcoming high.


Hopeful of the future adventures
Looking forward for all the ventures;
She waits for it all to keep getting better
Tho the time to come seems as bland as the weather.


The boat in her command
Is sailing in the sea;
With herself as the captain
She enjoys the journey with a tea.


She searched long for a company to join her boat;
Tho the people were too busy admiring the shore.
The journey to them was an impossible amore;
The trip to beyond was then her solo explore.


A beautiful smile on her face will always linger;
A passion to learn already made her a thinker.
Maybe she would just be a solo explorer;
But her journey would be worthwhile for a journal.
